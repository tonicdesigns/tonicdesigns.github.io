Mobile Ads

I created mobile graphics for a few mobile casino games. The goal for Cats vs Dogs Slots was to create a visually captivating mobile ad that could be used to draw in potential players. I integrated the main characters of the game to excite the players and to create a playful atmosphere. I played off the already complimentary color scheme by involving a red curtain to border the ad, and by giving the background a blue background to contrast the forefront better.

For Lucky Slots, the client wanted to create a pop-up window that displayed one of the game's promotion- the Rabbit's Foot. The focus of this window is divided into three distinct sections. The title and x-button are on top, then the actual item with a action blurb is in the middle, and the play now button is position bottom center. By keeping an analogous color scheme for the majority of the window, the play now button stands out as the eye-catching call-to-action button.
