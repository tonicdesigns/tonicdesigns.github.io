First Petal

A mythological being from Japanese folklore, the kitsune was characterized as a trickster. However, folklore also described them as kind and loving life partners, both dedicated and romantic. Kitsune were shape shifters and often used their power to appear as beautiful women. It's said that their tails signify their age, wisdom, and power. The more tails a Kitsune has, the more powerful it is.

I wanted to capture a kindred spirit, instead of the popular vision of a sly and untrustworthy spirit. The style is inspired by the art of Chinese papercutting, where traditional red paper was used to create bold and striking designs.
