Tynker

As a contract artist, I was in charge of populating and updating the media gallery with new and improved vector art assets to be used in student projects and teacher templates.

Tynker's products are meant for a wide range of ages, from elemetary school to high school. In order to design for a wide range of users, Tynker has adpated a different style guide to pertain to each age group. Part of my job was to develop these styles through my asset creation.
