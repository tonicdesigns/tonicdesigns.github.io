Nebulr

Nebulr is a product prototype that was conceptualized by a team and myself during Space Startup Weekend. Nebulr was designed to enhance the question and answering platform by connecting the professionals with the general public or students. Our team wanted to capture the intuitive user-experience that Tinder provides, while keeping the activity high by utilizing an appealing forum structure that rewards and allows votes for the best answers, similar to Reddit’s forums or StackOverflow.
