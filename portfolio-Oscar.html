Cat Barf

My cat, Oscar, has aged to the point of barfing up his meals. Unfortunately, we learned he had tumors in his stomach and cancer that had already spread. I created this to memorialize his excessive vomiting and to create a light-hearted portrait of my cat before he passed on. While he didn't hunt hamsters, snakes, and rabbits, I felt a little embellishing of his meal's ghosts would add some variety in composition and color.
