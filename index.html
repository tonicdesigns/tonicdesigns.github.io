How I Work

My design process

Research

Understand the end goals and direction for the project, including target style, colors, and platform.

Sketch

Brainstorm ideas by sketching them out to get a sense of visual direction and project scope.

Refine

Move designs into Illustrator or Photoshop and begin adding details and creating mock-ups.

Polish

Get feedback and polish designs by reiterating details and getting assets ready implementation.
